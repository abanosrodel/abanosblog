10K ROSES

More than just a tourist destination, Cordova's 10,000 Roses Café is an immersive experience that combines the beauty of nature with creativity and ingenuity. This sea of lit roses offers an enchanted experience that will stick with you long after you leave its shores, whether you're looking for a romantic meeting, a stunning backdrop for photography, or just a moment of peace amidst mesmerizing surroundings.

MIRACLE ART MUSEUM

The Miracle Art Museum in Cordova isn’t just a collection of paintings; it’s a canvas that invites you to step inside and become a part of the art. It’s a celebration of creativity, where visitors are encouraged to explore, engage, and leave with not just photographs but lasting memories of playful interactions with art that defies the boundaries of imagination.

Gilutongan Island in Cordova, Cebu

The Philippines' breathtaking natural beauty is exemplified by Gilutongan Island. Visitors to this peaceful sanctuary in Cordova, Cebu are left with unforgettable memories of its pristine scenery, abundant marine species, and friendly locals. It's an invitation to commune with nature in all its pristine form, not merely a means of getting away from the everyday grind.
